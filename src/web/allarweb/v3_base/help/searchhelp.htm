Help with:

Keyword Search:

Use the "Keyword Search" to quickly find specific files whose names you know.

Category Search:

Use the "Category Search" to narrow a search through a "drill-down" process. Choose keywords from the available fields and hit the search button. After each search the list of available keywords and fields will shrink until you reach a number of items you are comfortable sorting through, at which point you may hit the "Browse" link and view the remaining items. You may choose to browse at any tiume the link is available to you.

Common Elements

This space will display any keywords that are common among all remaining items in the drill-down search.
