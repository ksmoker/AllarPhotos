<div align="center"><h3>Adobe Acrobat Reader</h3></div>
<p>Some files on this site are saved in Adobe Portable Document Format (.pdf files). 
<p>This is a common file format for sharing documents across any computer platform thanks 
to Adobe's free Acrobat Reader software. In fact, you may already have this software installed on your computer.
<p>If you do not have a copy of this free software or if you desire the latest version, go to the 
<a href="http://www.adobe.com/products/acrobat/readstep.html" target="_blank">Acrobat download page</a> 
and make sure to select Acrobat Reader to download your copy.
