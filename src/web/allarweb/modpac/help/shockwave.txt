<div align="center"><h3>Macromedia Shockwave</h3></div>
<p>Shockwave is a multimedia technology often used in presentations and interactive, multimedia websites. 
<p>The Shockwave Player allows you to view files and web pages using Shockwave technology. In fact, you may already have this software installed on your computer.
<p>If you do not have a copy of this free software or if you desire the latest version, go to 
<a href="http://sdc.shockwave.com/shockwave/download/frameset.fhtml" target="_blank">Macromedia's Shockwave</a> 
and download your copy.
