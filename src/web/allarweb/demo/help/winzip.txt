<div align="center"><h3>WinZip</h3></div>
<p>If you use a Windows compatible computer, any files you download from 
this site will be saved in a zip archive (.zip files). 
<p>This is a very common method for packaging and compressing files for 
speedy and safe transmission over the internet. WinZip is just one of many widely available utility programs used to 
unpackage zip files. In fact, you may already have this software installed on your computer.
<p>If you do not have a copy of this shareware or if you desire the latest version, go to the 
<a href="http://www.winzip.com/" target="_blank">WinZip website</a> 
and download your copy. Installation is easy, you can even set up your browser to automatically decompress any 
.zip files you download.
